Looking for a new adventure? Try one of these creative challenges and test your skills!

What Are Minecraft Challenges?

Challenges are fun things that can make your gameplay fun. You can do them solo or with friends! Some challenges can be in creative or in survival, such as speedrunner VS hunter or can't kill mobs, those would be in survival and one of them can be solo. The goal of speedrunner vs hunters is the speedrunner to complete tasks white trying to avoid the hunters, and a group of players act as hunter trying to kill the speedrunner.

  • Create a redstone only base

  • Defeat the Ender Dragon with only iron gear
